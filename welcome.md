## Welcome to Banodoco!

To keep the quality of conversation high, this space is focused on people who are actively contributing to the open-source AI art ecosystem — whether that's:

• Making art and pushing creative boundaries with AI tools
• Contributing to open-source projects
• Sharing what you're learning publicly — notes, tutorials, breakdowns
• Helping others learn and grow

Anyone is welcome to browse and lurk — all the information here is open. But we have a gentle curation process for who can speak.

**Want to join the conversation?** Head over to <#1138861011206688829> and share a little about yourself — for example:

• Things you've made or contributed to the space
• Why you're passionate about open-source AI art
• What you're working on or want to contribute

If your intro shows you're relevant and interested, community members will upvote it and you'll be approved to post everywhere.
