## Welcome to Banodoco!

We're a community bringing together **open source AI model builders**, **engineers and tinkerers** expanding what these tools can do, and **ambitious artists** pushing the bounds of creative expression with AI.

Our goal is to create the most impactful open source community for controllable artistic creation — by connecting people who build models, people who build tools, and people who use both to make great work.

## Getting started & ways to contribute

**To get started:**
**1.** If you'd like to speak, see <#1478500886283030710> and introduce yourself in <#1138861011206688829> — tell us what you're working on or interested in
**2.** Explore the channels — jump into whatever catches your eye
**3.** Ask for help in <#1163250319107555388> if you get stuck — share as much context as possible

**Some ways to contribute:**
• **Help others** — even a quick answer in support can save someone hours
• **Share workflows & resources** — what seems simple to you might unlock something for someone else - see resources channels above.
• **Create & share art** — in <#1138865343314530324> or <#1225995744549408829>, your work inspires others about what's possible
• **Take on a project** — check <#1411778653443850411> for ideas, or start your own
• **Apply for a microgrant** — if you need compute, check out <#1479173703441846404>
